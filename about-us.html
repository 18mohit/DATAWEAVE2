Leadership Team

Leadership Team

 

Karthik Bettadapura

Co-founder & CEO

Karthik has over 10 years of technology leadership experience and has built and managed large-scale Web and data analytics products and platforms. Prior to co-founding DataWeave in 2011, where he drives the company's vision and business development strategy, he was part of the team that built CNBC's online property called Web18. He holds a MTech degree from IIIT–Bangalore, where his research focused on Information Retrieval, Advanced Databases, and Distributed Systems.

 

Vikranth Ramanolla

Co-founder & CTO

Vikranth has over 8 years of experience building big data platforms and large-scale unstructured data aggregation and analysis engines. Before he co-Founded DataWeave, he was part of the team at Web18 that built and managed one of India’s premier Web properties. He holds a MTech from IIIT–Bangalore with a focus on Information Retrieval, Advanced Operating Systems and Web Services.

 

Krishnan Thyagarajan

President & COO

Krish is a global executive with 25+ years in IT industry. He has significant experience running businesses across geographies, cultures and maturity levels. Before DataWeave, Krish worked in leadership roles at Dell, Quest Software, and Microsoft, among others. His areas of expertise include P&L management, market and enterprise customers development, nurturing partner ecosystems, and incubation of new businesses.
